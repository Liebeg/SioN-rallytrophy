Multiplayer guide

Step 1:

Go download Hamachi and install it

Step 2:

Make an account in hamachi or log in to an existing one

Step 3:

Click the "power on" button to start

Step 4 (Making the server):

Open the "Network" tab at the top and "create a new network"

Step 5 (Making the server):

Put the network name in the "Network ID" box and give it a password if you want to

Step 6 (Making the server):

Give the network id and password to whoever you want to play with

Step 7 (Making the server):

Open "RallyTrophyLobby.exe"

Step 8 (Making the server):

Give yourself a name, change the settings and click "create game"

Step 4 (Joining the server):

Open the "Network" tab at the top and "join an existing network"

Step 5 (Joining the server):

Put in the information your friend gave you

Step 6 (Joining the server):

Open the "Network" tab at the top and "join an existing network"

Step 7 (Joining the server):

Open "RallyTrophyLobby.exe"

Step 8 (Joining the server):

Give yourself a name and click "Local search" and after it finds the session your friend made just click "join game"
